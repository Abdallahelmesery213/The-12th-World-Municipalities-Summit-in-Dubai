Greetings,

Datamatix takes this opportunity to extend its sincere appreciation to global states, provinces, governorates, cities, local governments, the business industry, and all other related organizations for their significant contributions toward developing integrated, sustainable cities that remain competitive in a rapidly changing world. As the world becomes increasingly interconnected, the challenge of building sustainable cities requires a strong foundation of governance, infrastructure, public services, innovation, and more.

From ensuring economic stability and efficient urban planning to providing accessible healthcare, education, and digital transformation, your organizations play a critical role in shaping the cities of tomorrow. You are at the forefront of creating inclusive and future-ready communities.

In this context, Datamatix, in collaboration with leading international institutions and key stakeholders from the public and private sectors, is pleased to present the 12th World Municipalities Summit, which will be held from June 2-5, 2025, in Dubai, UAE. The summit will bring together global leaders and decision-makers to discuss the latest developments in municipal innovation and urban transformation. In this regard, the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute will honor the contributions of outstanding global municipalities and city leaders during the summit.

On this esteemed occasion, we invite your organization's active participation in this initiative to share your extraordinary achievements, provide invaluable insights, and help shape the future of our communities. Together, we can support the "Year of Community." This event offers a unique platform for exchanging best practices and fostering innovation to drive progress and realize the vision of the 2025 "Year of Community."

Your organization's participation as a Strategic Partner will play a vital role in supporting this initiative and celebrating the collaboration between the public and private sectors globally.

Given the limited time, we would greatly appreciate the early completion of participation and partnership procedures to facilitate your organization's involvement in the event's activities.

We look forward to your esteemed institution's involvement in shaping this significant global initiative. Datamatix welcomes your organization's leadership and top management to participate in this global event, contributing to the knowledge exchange that will define the next era of municipal excellence.

Your engagement will help shape actionable strategies and inspire international collaborations that drive the development of dynamic, sustainable, and people-centric cities.

Sincerely,
Ali A. Kamali
Managing Director
